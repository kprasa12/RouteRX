What it does

Built for clinic coordinators

No GIS expertise needed. Enter a ZIP code, choose how many stops, and RouteRX handles the rest.

📍

Need-based stop selection

Automatically identifies the highest-need ZIP codes near your starting point using population and health access data.

🗺️

Optimized routing

Powered by OpenRouteService to calculate the shortest drive path across all your stops — saving fuel and time.

📊

Visual map + route sheet

See your full route on an interactive map alongside a printable table of stops with distance and need scores.

📥

CSV download

Export the full route as a CSV file to share with drivers, volunteers, or import into scheduling tools.

🇺🇸

All 50 states covered

Works for any ZIP code across the continental United States — from dense cities to rural counties.

⚡

Results in seconds

No login, no setup. Enter your ZIP, pick your stops, hit generate — your route is ready in under 10 seconds.

How it works

From ZIP code to optimized route in four simple steps.

1

Enter your starting ZIP

Type in the ZIP code where your clinic vehicle starts the day.

2

Choose number of stops

Pick how many communities you want to visit — up to 10 stops per route.

3

We rank the best ZIPs

RouteRX finds nearby ZIP codes ranked by health access need and population.

4

Get your route

An optimized map and stop list is returned — ready to print or export.
